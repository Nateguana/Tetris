CONTROLS

- A: move tetromino to the left

- D: move tetromino to the right

- S: make tetromino fall faster

- Spacebar: instantly drop tetromino into place

- Q: rotate tetromino

- E: rotate tetromino

- Esc: exit game

- P: open pause menu or return to start menu
